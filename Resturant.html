Reservations are accepted for the dining room only. Call 970-453-9491 for availability. Due to our unpredictable weather patterns, we do not take reservations for our outdoor patio space.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much do you charge to host an event?

We charge a site fee, equal to your non-refundable deposit, to privately rent either the Dining room or outdoor patio of our establishment. Site fees, as well as food & beverage minimums, vary between your preferred time frame, season and day of the week. For example it will be less expense for us to host your group and close our dining room during dinner on a weeknight versus a Friday or Saturday. If you choose to host a luncheon with us during our quiet hours, our fees will be less expensive than they would be for a dinner event. Please inquire and we would be happy to discuss this in detail!

What kind of food do you offer at the Himalayan Curry and Kebob for private events?

We have plenty of food options to fit any occasion and most budgets.
Please contact us for more information and sample menus!

What is the guest capacity for a private event?

50 total guests

Can we have an event in the upstairs Bar?

We are grateful to co-habitate with our overnight guests and surround ourselves with our locals, therefore, our Bar will always be open to the public and is not available for private events.
